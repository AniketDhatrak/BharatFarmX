National Cooperative Development Corporation

A statutory corporation under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, offering financial assistance to cooperatives, farmers, and rural entrepreneurs.

Rural Electrification Corporation Limited

A Navratna company under the Ministry of Power, financing and promoting rural electrification projects, and providing assistance to farmers for irrigation and other agricultural activities.

Small Farmers Agribusiness Consortium

A society under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, working towards enhancing the competitiveness of small farmers and promoting agribusiness.

National Agricultural Cooperative Marketing Federation of India Ltd.

A cooperative marketing organization under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, working towards ensuring remunerative prices to farmers for their produce by providing them with marketing support and undertaking procurement and distribution activities.

National Horticulture Board

An autonomous body under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, promoting integrated development of horticulture and providing financial assistance to horticulture-related projects.

National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development

A development finance institution under the Ministry of Finance, promoting agriculture and rural development by providing credit, technical assistance, and other support services to farmers and rural entrepreneurs.

National Bureau of Fish Genetic Resources

An autonomous institute under the Indian Council of Agricultural Research, working towards conservation, characterisation, and utilisation of fish genetic resources in the country.

National Institute of Cooperative Development

An autonomous institute under the Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, providing training, research, and consultancy services to cooperatives and other stakeholders in the cooperative sector.

Reserve Bank of India

The central bank of the country, regulating and supervising the financial system, and providing credit and other support services to farmers and rural entrepreneurs through its various schemes and programmes.

Small Industries Development Bank of India

A development finance institution under the Ministry of Finance, promoting and financing small-scale industries and businesses, including those in the agriculture and rural sectors.

Skymet Weather Services Pvt. Ltd.

A private weather forecasting company, providing weather-related information and services to farmers and other stakeholders in the agriculture and rural sectors.

Tribal Cooperative Marketing DevelopmentFederation of India

An apex organization under the Ministry of Tribal Affairs, working towards the socio-economic development of tribal communities by promoting and marketing their tribal art and craft products.

Department of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of Uttar Pradesh

The agriculture department of the state government of Uttar Pradesh, responsible for promoting and implementing various schemes and programmes for the development of agriculture and farmers in the state.

Uttar Pradesh State Agricultural Marketing Board

The state agency responsible for regulating and promoting agricultural marketing in Uttar Pradesh, providing market infrastructure, facilities, and services to farmers and traders.

Vision India Foundation

A non-profit organization working towards the socio-economic development of the country through various initiatives, including those in the agriculture and rural sectors.

Yes Bank

A private sector bank providing banking and financial services to farmers and rural entrepreneurs through its various schemes and programmes.
